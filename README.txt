# Pig game with dice in the browser

# Rules
Player take turn to roll a dice. If the dice is *not* 1, the number is added to the current score of the player. The player can roll more times, but if the dice shows 1, the player loses all of his current score and the players switch. 
If the player decides to hold, his current score is added to the player's global score and the players switch. 

The goal is to reach the defined winning score before your opponent.
